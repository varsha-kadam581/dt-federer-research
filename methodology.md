# Methodology

## City Selected
Hyderabad

## Segments Selected
- Specialty Biotech
- Diagnostics & Life-Science Tools

## Research Sources
- LinkedIn
- Company websites
- Tracxn
- Startup India
- Genome Valley ecosystem
- Google Search

## Research Process
1. Created raw list of 70 companies
2. Removed traders/service companies
3. Checked manufacturing capability
4. Verified technical founders
5. Checked growth signals
6. Assigned Federer score
7. Shortlisted final PASS companies

## AI Usage
AI tools were used for:
- company discovery
- summarizing company profiles
- identifying growth signals

Human verification was used for:
- manufacturer validation
- founder background checks
- final Federer scoring
- PASS/FAIL decisions

## Learnings
Specialty biotech and diagnostics companies in Hyderabad often show strong R&D orientation, export growth, and technically driven leadership, making them strong Federer-fit candidates.
